Citadel of the Seraphim

Open the campaign map, then click an unlocked mountain level to begin a battle.

Training Battle The demons are crossing from the broken citadel toward the holy gate. Build a Lightspire tower, then move Alizel's Host onto the road to hold the lane. The tutorial pauses the first wave until each core action is clear. After the wave, you will see what you earned and choose the next battle or the Armory.

The Breach

The mountain road has broken open.

Demons pour from the corrupted citadel and climb toward the gate, forcing the hosts to fight for every road.

The Climb

Sixteen battles mark the lower mountain.

Some roads lead straight upward. Others branch into sidequests that can recover tower patterns and new options.

The Armory

Radiance strengthens what survives.

Victories earn Radiance, while special objectives grant Sanctum Cores for rarer upgrades and evolutions.

The Gate

Hold the road until the hosts can reach the summit.

Towers build tension in place. Angel hosts move to danger points. Named angels can turn a battle once.
